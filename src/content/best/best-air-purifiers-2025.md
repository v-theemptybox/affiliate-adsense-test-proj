Air quality inside your home is often 2–5x worse than outside. A good air purifier changes that. Here's what actually works.

## Why Air Quality Matters at Home

Common indoor pollutants:
- **Pet dander and hair** — triggers allergies even if you're not "allergic to pets"
- **VOCs** — off-gassed from furniture, paints, cleaning products
- **Cooking smoke** — fine particulate matter from cooking
- **Dust mites** — thrive in bedding and upholstery

A HEPA filter captures 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns and larger. That covers all of the above.
