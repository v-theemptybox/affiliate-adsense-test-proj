## Best Overall: iRobot Roomba j7+

Read our [full Roomba j7+ review](/reviews/irobot-roomba-j7-plus) — the short version: the obstacle avoidance is the best we've tested, the self-emptying base is transformative, and it handles pet hair without drama.

The j7+ is the robot vacuum to recommend without caveats for most homes.

[Check Price](/go/roomba-j7-plus)

## Best Budget: Eufy RoboVac 11S

At $149, the 11S is the thinnest robot vacuum available (2.85") and passes under most sofas. No mapping, no app control — it bounces around randomly — but it picks up debris reliably on hardwood and low-pile carpet.

For apartment dwellers with simple floor plans, it's hard to argue against the value.

[Check Price](/go/eufy-robovac-11s)

## Best for Pet Hair: Shark AI ULTRA

The Shark AI ULTRA's Matrix Clean Home Mapping covers every square inch in a grid pattern rather than randomly, making it exceptionally thorough on carpets. The self-cleaning brushroll prevents pet hair from tangling — a genuine differentiator.

[Check Price](/go/shark-ai-ultra)

## Best Mapping: Roborock S8 Pro Ultra

The S8 Pro Ultra is the premium option with LiDAR navigation so precise it can clean around individual chair legs. It also mops, self-empties, and self-washes the mop pad. Overkill for most people, but unmatched for large homes or those who want set-and-forget perfection.

[Check Price](/go/roborock-s8-pro-ultra)

## How We Tested

Each robot vacuum completed 30 test runs across identical floor types. We measured:
- Debris pickup rate on hardwood vs carpet
- Navigation efficiency (time to cover full area)
- Obstacle avoidance capability
- App reliability and ease of setup
- Noise levels at 1 meter distance
